About Me

Who I Am

Hello, I'm Miko! I'm a photographer, web developer, and content creator. Currently I am studying Digital Media - Web Design at the University of Central Florida. I like to experiment and mix many digital mediums to create compelling and unique digital creations for my clients and for myself.

My Work

Photography for me has become more than just taking photos, applying filters, and posting to social media. It has become a medium to tell stories. I prefer to capture the wild, the unscripted moments because they have the potential to tell genuine stories. Stories that are real and can only be captured once.
